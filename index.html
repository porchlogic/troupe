We will start at 6:00pm at Lucky Lab by setting up our audio nodes for a low-key drum circle. We'll then go out on a short tour through the city.


This is phase 2 of building the cybernetic mobile venue. We are testing the 3D distributed sound system where each one of our speakers is a node that plays a different part of the music. DJs will be able to play the system live, as if we are a mobile venue.

What to bring:

speaker + phone

This is the easiest way to participate. Simply open up pulse.porchlogic.com and select your role to join the band.


speaker + iOS device

This is the intermediate level. Get the Dialekt or Drumcomputer app, and come 15 minutes early for a tutorial.


speaker + USB MIDI gear

This is for those who have music gear and want to join in. Visit pulse.porchlogic.com to see the song format to program to.
